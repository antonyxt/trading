This video, "The 2 Candles That Tell You Everything," is the second episode of Arjo's "Pips to Payouts" series. It focuses on **Candle Science**, a method of predicting future price action by analyzing the relationship between individual candles and institutional PD (Premium/Discount) Arrays.

### **Core Concept: Candle Science**
Candle science is based on the idea that every single candlestick is actually a collection of **Fair Value Gaps (FVGs)** on a lower time frame [[01:08](http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=YZPD-TAFNuk&t=68)]. By understanding how one candle closes relative to a PD Array, you can predict the direction of the next candle with high accuracy.

### **The Two Types of Candles**
Arjo categorizes candles into two functional types [[06:19](http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=YZPD-TAFNuk&t=379)]:
* **Respect Candles:** Characterized by long wicks(AKA rejections from PDA) (at the bottom for bullish, top for bearish). These indicate a reversal on lower time frames and show that a PD Array is holding [[06:44](http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=YZPD-TAFNuk&t=404)].
* **Disrespect Candles:** Characterized by large bodies and tiny wicks. these indicate strong momentum and a continuation of the current trend [[08:17](http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=YZPD-TAFNuk&t=497)].


### **Two-Candle Rejection Rule**
This is a specific framework used to determine how long it takes for a price to react to a PD Array [[11:07](http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=YZPD-TAFNuk&t=667)]:
1.  **Immediate Rejection:** The first candle to hit the PD Array is a "Respect Candle" and immediately bounces away [[11:34](http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=YZPD-TAFNuk&t=694)].
2.  **Secondary Rejection:** The first candle fails to reject, but the second candle sweeps the first candle's liquidity (low/high) and then rejects [[11:54](http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=YZPD-TAFNuk&t=714)].
3.  **Ugly Rejection:** If it takes more than two candles to reject, it is considered a **consolidation**, and momentum is likely lost. Traders are advised to avoid these setups [[18:05](http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=YZPD-TAFNuk&t=1085)].

### **Defining a Trend Objectively**
The video provides a mechanical way to identify market bias [[02:44](http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=YZPD-TAFNuk&t=164)]:
* **Bullish Trend:** Discount arrays (bullish FVGs, swing lows) are **respected**, while Premium arrays (bearish FVGs, swing highs) are **disrespected** [[03:03](http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=YZPD-TAFNuk&t=183)].
* **Bearish Trend:** Premium arrays are respected, while Discount arrays are disrespected [[03:52](http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=YZPD-TAFNuk&t=232)].

### **Practical Application: The Rejection High/Low**
To confirm if a PD Array is being "disrespected," Arjo looks for price to "run" the **Rejection High/Low** [[19:06](http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=YZPD-TAFNuk&t=1146)]. 
* If price is approaching a target but a bearish FVG is in the way, you wait for price to trade above the "Rejection High" of that FVG. 
* Once that high is "run," the resistance is considered broken, giving you the **clarity** to enter a trade toward your higher time-frame target [[21:49](http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=YZPD-TAFNuk&t=1309)].
