# 📊 DAILY TRADING PLAYBOOK (SMC + HTF BIAS)

## 1. BIAS (Top-Down)

* Monthly + Weekly + Daily aligned → ✅ Trade
* Not aligned → ❌ No trade

---

## 2. HTF ZONE (1H)

Mark ONLY ONE clean PD array:

* FVG OR OB OR Liquidity level

Zone must have:

* Strong displacement (fast move away)
* Liquidity sweep before move
* Clear structure break (BOS)
* Prefer FVG presence
* Fresh (not retested multiple times)

👉 If weak zone → SKIP

---

## 3. WAIT

* Let price ENTER the zone
* No anticipation

---

## 4. LTF CONFIRMATION (5 min)

### ✅ TRADE ONLY IF REJECTION:

* Strong rejection candle (wick)
* OR engulfing
* OR strong displacement away

👉 Entry = break of that candle high/low

---

### ❌ NO TRADE IF ACCEPTANCE:

* Small candles inside zone
* Slow movement through zone
* No strong reaction

---

## 5. EXECUTION

### BUY:

* HTF bullish
* Price in discount zone
* LTF rejection
* Enter on break of high

### SELL:

* Opposite

---

## 6. STOP LOSS

* Below/above swing or liquidity sweep

---

## 7. TARGET

* Next PD array in direction

---

## ⚠️ RULES (NON-NEGOTIABLE)

* No HTF alignment → No trade
* No strong zone → No trade
* No rejection → No trade
* No over-marking zones
* First touch is best

---

## 🧠 CORE EDGE

"Trade reaction at important zones — not the zone itself"
