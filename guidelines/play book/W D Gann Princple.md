# 📘 Gann Fundamentals (Core Notes)

## 🧱 3 Pillars of Gann

### 1. Balance (Equilibrium)
**Foundation:**
Price ≈ Time  

- When price movement equals time movement:
  - Market reaches **balance**
  - **Reaction becomes likely**

---

### 2. Trend Has Geometry
- Market does NOT move randomly
- Trends move at **specific rates (angles)**

| Trend Type | Angle |
|------------|------|
| Slow trend | Shallow |
| Fast trend | Steep |

👉 Trend strength = **speed of price relative to time**

---

### 3. Time Governs Change
- Price does NOT reverse randomly  
- Reversal happens when **time conditions are met**

---

## 🧠 Core Thinking (Always Ask)

1. Is price moving **fast or slow relative to time**?  
2. Has the move lasted **too long (time exhaustion)**?  
3. Is the market approaching **balance (Price ≈ Time)**?  

---

# ⚡ Market Behavior Classification (Gann View)

## 🧠 1. Fast Move (Imbalance)

**Definition:**  
High price change in **few bars** (high velocity)

**Characteristics:**
- Large candle bodies  
- Minimal pullback  
- Often forms imbalance (e.g., FVG)  
- Can be bullish or bearish  

**Gann Meaning:**
- Market is **out of equilibrium**
- Move is **unsustainable**
- Likely followed by:
  - Correction OR
  - Consolidation  

---

## 🧠 2. Balanced Move (Equilibrium) ⭐ MOST IMPORTANT

**Definition:**  
Price progresses **proportionally with time**  
(Two-sided participation)

**Characteristics:**
- Mixed bullish & bearish candles  
- Wicks on both sides  
- Gradual progression  
- Candle overlap  

**Gann Meaning:**
Price ≈ Time  

- Market is in **equilibrium**
- Trend is **healthy & sustainable**

---

## 🧠 3. Consolidation (Time Dominance)

**Definition:**  
Time passes but **price makes little net progress**

**Characteristics:**
- Sideways movement  
- Range-bound  
- Repeated highs/lows  
- Can include false breakouts  
- Often within “master candles”  

**Gann Meaning:**
- **Time active, price inactive**
- Energy accumulation phase  
- Leads to future expansion  

---

# 🔷 Real Classification Table

| Type            | Price Movement | Time Taken   | Meaning       |
|-----------------|--------------|-------------|--------------|
| Fast Move       | Large        | Small       | Imbalance     |
| Balanced Move   | Proportional | Proportional| Equilibrium   |
| Consolidation   | Small        | Large       | Compression   |

---

# 🔥 Key Insights (High Value)

- Fast move → **Imbalance → Not sustainable**  
- Consolidation → **Time build-up → Expansion coming**  
- Balanced move → **True trend (most reliable)**  

---

# ⚠️ Important Notes

- Direction ≠ Speed  
  - Fast ≠ Bullish  
  - Fast can be **up or down**

- Wicks / FVG are **symptoms**, not definitions  

- Focus on:
  - **Price-Time relationship**
  - Not just candle patterns  

---

# 🧭 Final Mental Model

Market cycles between:

1. **Imbalance (Fast Move)**  
2. **Balance (Trend)**  
3. **Compression (Consolidation)**  

👉 Repeat cycle

🛠️ Practical Steps
Log scale ON
Weekly timeframe fixed
Fix chart window (don’t keep zooming)
Select major swing
Draw 1x1 (natural slope)
Observe behavior relative to it

When market structure changes → re-anchor Gann Fan
When structure is intact → keep using the same anchor
Gann is not fixed to price — it is fixed to structure
Gann anchor = valid only as long as structure is valid
Sometimes:

Market doesn’t fully break structure
But starts deviating from 1x1 gradually

👉 This is called:

Structure weakening

That’s your early warning signal to prepare for re-anchoring.
